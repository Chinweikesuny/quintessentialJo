About us

TQJ clothing brand started officially in December, 2021. Before the launch of the brand, a period of 5 years was spent in gaining experience, learning and relearning and mastering the skill perfectly.
A brand that springs out of an intense desire to create classy, creative and gorgeous outfits for diverse races, colours and personalities.
We believe in the uniqueness of every humans body as well as their distinct tastes in fabric, clothes and style. The Quintessential Jo simply fits into these choices and make them realities, thereby creating the unimaginable and perfect fit for all.
